Chicken

Ah, chicken!

Chicken is the true test of a commercial kitchen. Achieving the right balance of taste and texture is what separates the top kitchens from the pretenders.

At Food LLC, we use a variety of methods to prepare chicken for our customers. Our in-house favorite, and that of many of our customers, is the stovetop-to-oven method - five minutes in a hot cast-iron skillet with a couple teaspoons of oil, followed by 10 minutes in a 425%deg oven. Packaged and delivered to your door, just in time for dinner.

For our packages of seasoned chicken, we marinate fresh chicken breast, seal and cook in our sous vide machine at 150%deg for 90 minutes, then chill in an ice bath until ready to refrigerate. Our special sous vide can be stored for several days, so order ahead for weekday dinners!

Beef

At Food LLC, we use the well-known 'sous vide' process to cook all the beef we sell.

Beef prepared using the sous vide process is seasoned and vacuum-sealed in an air-tight plastic bag. Each bag is then immersed in a water bath whose temperature is precisely controlled, within a fraction of a degree.

Sous vide cooking takes place at a lower temperature but for a longer time than traditional forms of cooking. For example, our lean beef shoulder is cooked at 130° for 24 hours. This produces a cut that is as tender as a more expensive beef roast, but without the drying of oven-roasting.
